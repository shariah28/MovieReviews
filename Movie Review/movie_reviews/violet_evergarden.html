Violet Evergarden Movie

Directed by Taichi Ishidate

Watch the Trailer!

Summary

Violet Evergarden: The Movie is a Japanese animated drama film produced by Kyoto Animation, directed by Taichi Ishidate and written by Reiko Yoshida, featuring character designs by Akiko Takase and music by Evan Call. The movie follows the story of Violet Evergarden as she continues to live her life after the end of the anime series, and her encounter with a young boy named Isabella York.

Characters

  • Violet Evergarden
  • Claudia Hodgins
  • Gilbert Bougainvillea
  • Cattleya Baudelaire
  • Benedict Blue
