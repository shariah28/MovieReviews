A Silent Voice

Directed by Naoko Yamada

Watch the Trailer!

Summary

A Silent Voice is a Japanese animated teen drama film produced by Kyoto Animation, directed by Naoko Yamada and written by Reiko Yoshida, featuring character designs by Futoshi Nishiya and music by Kensuke Ushio. The movie follows the story of a young man named Shouya Ishida, who tries to make amends with a deaf girl named Shouko Nishimiya whom he had bullied in elementary school.

Characters

  • Shouya Ishida
  • Shouko Nishimiya
  • Miyoko Sahara
  • Naoka Ueno
  • Yuzuru Kawai
