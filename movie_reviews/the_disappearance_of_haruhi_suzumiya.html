Summary

The Disappearance of Haruhi Suzumiya is a Japanese animated science-fiction film produced by Kyoto Animation, directed by Tatsuya Ishihara and written by Fumihiko Shimo, based on the light novel series of the same name by Nagaru Tanigawa. The movie follows the story of high school student Kyon as he wakes up one day to find that his classmate and friend Haruhi Suzumiya has disappeared, along with all traces of her existence.

Characters
