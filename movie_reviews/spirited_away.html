Summary

Spirited Away is a Japanese animated film directed by Hayao Miyazaki and produced by Studio Ghibli. The movie tells the story of a young girl named Chihiro, who finds herself trapped in a magical world of spirits and supernatural beings, and her quest to find her way back home.
