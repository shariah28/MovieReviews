Wolf Children

Directed by Mamoru Hosoda

Watch the Trailer!

Summary

Wolf Children is a Japanese animated drama film produced by Studio Chizu, directed by Mamoru Hosoda and written by Hosoda and Satoko Okudera. The movie tells the story of a young woman named Hana who falls in love with a man who can transform into a wolf. They have two children who also have the ability to transform into wolves and must navigate the challenges of raising a family of "wolf children" on their own.

Characters

  • Hana
  • Ame
  • Yuki
  • Ookami
  • Souhei
